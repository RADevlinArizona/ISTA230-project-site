ABOUT US

20 Years ago we both graduated here in Tucson from the Eller College of Management, and quickly started our careers in the restaurant industry. After much traveling we found ourselves back in Tucson with a passion for delivering great dining experiences to patrons. With the experience we have gained throughout our careers we decided to finally open a place we could call our own, right here in Tucson. We hope you join us on this journey, and come to call Wildcat Cafe one of your favorite spots in town.
